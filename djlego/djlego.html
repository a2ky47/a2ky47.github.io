    Design Process

    This is a creative exercise for concept development as well as rapid prototyping. We were asked to come up with a word that we are interested and combine with other student randomly and then create concepts and produce products. My partnet and I work on "Toys" and "DJ". We started our project from 1 dimetional ideas based on that topic, and with many illterations and user testing we quickely transformed our ideas into a product that be intereacted by users.
