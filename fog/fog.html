    Introduction

    It's a narrative between a couple－Kate and Alex, and the player is Kate. The story begins with good memory of relationship and then Alex start to emotional blackmail Kate. There are 3 endings based on your choice in terms of how many times you be manipulated and how ridicoulous that you accept the demand.

    Research

    Definition

    “A person use fear, obligation, and guilt to manipulate you” from Susan Forward.

    The formula of emotional blackmail is “ If you don’t do what I want you to do, you will suffer ”. It is the typical method to threaten you in order to let you do the things they want you to do. In the process, they ignore why you don’t want to do, and makes you feel guilt or fear. You may want to maintain the relationship or ease the tension at the moment or not to be degraded comply the other.

    STOP

    Give yourselves time to think about it. If the person keep asking, answer like “jump of needle” “ alright, I will think about it”

    LISTEN

    Ask yourself what do you feel, and think about whether you want/should be treated like this

    RESPONSE

    Response after you think more, and talk in a calm down way

    Concept

    My intention is to let people have more awareness of emotional blackmail and encourage people to break the situation or try not to be the emotional blackmailer.

    In the first phase, I was thinking to build an installation to show the pattern of emotional blackmail and ask people to break it. Then, I realized by only see the pattern and break it visually is not really feeling this concept.

    Thus, I decide to write a story and make people engage in. The idea is to make people practice not to be manipulated and know it's right to refuse. The game indicates that if you refuse the unreasonable request can actually lead to a healthier relationship
