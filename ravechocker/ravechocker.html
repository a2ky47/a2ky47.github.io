    Inspiration

    pictures from google image

    People wants to be cool in the music festival, such as Ultra, Coachella, etc. The most popular outfits are with shinny, bling accessories. Although music festival are very high in average, sometimes, people might be a little bit awkward to jump or follow the beats because they go with acquaintance instead of their best friend, or people might not ready to join. In other cases, people might be too high in concert.



    Intention

    1. Make people cool in music festival
    2. Encourage them engaged in music festival more
    3. Provide a way to warn people if their heartbeats are not normal.
